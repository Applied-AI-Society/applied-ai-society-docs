## Overview

New concept page at `docs/concepts/game-design.md` in the AAS public docs. Positioned as the umbrella discipline that context engineering and intent engineering are components of. sidebar_position: 3 (between context engineering and liberation architecture).

## Content Structure

1. **Opening** — The player-to-coach shift. Jordan Hall framework. When AI handles execution, humans design the game.
2. **What is a game?** — Four components: objectives, rules, guardrails, scoring.
3. **The Naismith principle** — The basketball analogy. Design the game, don't play it.
4. **Why this is the meta-skill** — Context engineering + intent engineering = game design. The umbrella.
5. **For practitioners** — When you help a client deploy AI, you're designing a game.
6. **Further Reading** — Links to intent engineering, context engineering, and AI Survival Guide source material.
